Pathfinder 2e Adventure: Caravan Defense

The Quest

Talking to our guild's goblin quest giver, we received a quest to protect a caravan traveling into town. Our party included Renee, a Rougarou Commander with the ability to transform into a wolf. Her prowess would prove vital in the upcoming battles.

[Image Placeholder: Goblin quest giver in a guild hall.]

Journey to the Caravan

Moving toward the caravan's route, a scouting team moved ahead to check for threats. Soon, we encountered a pack of hobgoblins lying in wait. Half of the hobgoblins reacted to the caravan's arrival, while the rest remained in position. This was our chance to strike.

[Image Placeholder: Dense forest path with a caravan approaching.]

The Battle Begins

The battle erupted as we ambushed the remaining hobgoblins. The Laughing Magus unleashed a dimensional assault, clashing with the hobgoblin warriors and their leader wielding a glaive. Renee, in her half-wolf form, sliced one hobgoblin warrior clean in half. Despite our efforts, my initial Chilling Darkness spell missed its mark.

[Image Placeholder: Hobgoblins ambushed by a Rougarou and a Laughing Magus.]

Turning the Tide

I cast Concordant Choir, shattering a hobgoblin warrior's armor, though he still stood. The Magus used Arcane Cascade followed by Thunderstrike, while Renee cast Form Up to bolster the team's movement. The hobgoblin archers targeted me and Renee but failed to land their shots.

[Image Placeholder: Magus with flaming sword attacking hobgoblin leader.]

The Hobgoblin General

The Hobgoblin General unleashed Fear on the Laughing Magus, but he retaliated with a fiery Spell Strike, igniting his sword and critically wounding a hobgoblin. When the General struck down the Magus, Renee stabilized him with her med kit, allowing him to rise again. I cast Purifying Icicle, striking the General and finishing him off.

[Image Placeholder: Hobgoblin General facing off against the party.]

The Final Push

Renee flanked and killed a hobgoblin archer with a ferocious bite, while the Magus critically hit another, destroying its armor and cleaving through its chest. The last hobgoblin's attack backfired, injuring itself, before the Magus, empowered by Renee's spell, struck it down with a flaming blade, ending the skirmish.

[Image Placeholder: Last hobgoblin falling as Magus delivers final blow.]

Victory and Return

Renee transformed into her full wolf form to chase after the caravan. However, the caravan team had already dealt with the remaining hobgoblins. With the threat eliminated, we escorted the caravan safely back to town, earning the guild's praise.

[Image Placeholder: Caravan arriving safely in town at sunset.]
